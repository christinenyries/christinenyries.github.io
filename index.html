About Christine

Christine is a software engineer who enjoys building well-structured code and learning new things.

She first got interested in programming when she learned Visual Basic during her third year in high school.She has enjoyed programming ever since. She discovered HTML&CSS afterwards and eventually joined her school’s computer club.

Christine got a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science in the University of Santo Tomas. She took her internship in a web development company where she became a full-stack developer building websites for clients. After graduation, she got the opportunity to work for a telecommunications company as an R&D engineer. There she got accustomed with various programming languages and learned the art of structuring code and designing tests. She joined a great team whom she is happy to plan user stories, debug problematic code, and play board games with during Sprint Review meetings.

When she’s not programming, Christine is in the kitchen figuring out which meal to cook for the day, reading an Isekai manga, or experimenting with Adobe Illustrator.
